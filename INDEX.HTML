TAXTILES &MODERAN TECHNOLOGYS

The organized textile industry is characterized
by the use of capital-intensive technology for mass production of textile products
and includes spinning, weaving, processing, and apparel manufacturing

Technology in Textile Industry :
study of basic principles using some scientific and engineering methodologies
that are concerned with the refining and manufacturing of all kinds of
textile fabrics and yarns using textile fibers is known as textile technology.
